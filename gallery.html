How We Plan TechX — The Strategy Room

TechX 2026 has been in the making for over 8 months. Our core team of 50+ volunteers started with brainstorming sessions in January, drafted event blueprints by March, and started reaching out to sponsors by May.



Every event goes through 3 rounds of review before being approved. From logistics to creative design, nothing is left to chance.


Stage Setup & Technical Infrastructure

Setting up a tech fest involves 200+ meters of cabling, custom-built LED panels, and a sound system capable of reaching 5000+ people in an open arena.



Our tech crew works non-stop for 72 hours before the event to ensure every projector, microphone, and network switch is perfectly configured.


Hackathon — Problem Statement Creation

Our hackathon problem statements are crafted in collaboration with industry partners including Google, Microsoft, and local startups. Each problem is designed to be:

  • Solvable within 36 hours
  • Relevant to real-world issues
  • Testable with a working prototype


Last year's winning project was an AI-powered crop disease detector that achieved 94% accuracy using just smartphone images.
